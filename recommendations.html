Here are some things that Máire and I like in the area in case you have some extra time to explore.

Wineries we <3 in Napa and Sonoma

  • Hall Winery--the vines that surround Branch House are sold directly to Hall plus we will taste some with our dinner
  • Unti--an old favorite for many years and one of the first organic wineries in Sonoma County featuring mostly Itlaian verietals. We will also drink some of these wines during our celebration.
  • Dry Creek Vineyards--great outdoor space and good whites!!
  • Cheateu Montelena --the first wine to beat the old world vines in 1977 in Paris, added bonus Irish connection

Fun Activities in Sonoma

  • Biking along Dry Creek Valley or from Napa to Yountville
  • Hiking Mount Helena or around Lake Sonoma
